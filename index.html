Game Description

Mind Logic is a puzzle game that challenges your reasoning, probability knowledge, and logical thinking skills.

In the first challenge, you will analyze a mystery bag filled with colored balls and determine the correct probabilities when drawing them randomly.

In the second challenge, you must analyze logical clues to determine the profession of three characters: Ana, Ben, and Cara.

Think carefully, analyze the clues, and choose the correct answers to achieve the highest score!
